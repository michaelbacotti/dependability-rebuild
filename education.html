Options Education is the foundational layer of everything we publish at Dependability Holdings LLC. If you're new to options, start here. If you're an experienced trader who wants to tighten your mental models, you'll find something useful here too.

The curriculum covers core concepts, the Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega), and strategy foundations — written for traders who want to use options deliberately, not just execute them. Glossary terms are linked inline, and every strategy piece includes a plain-language explanation of the risk profile.

New education pieces are added as we identify gaps in the existing curriculum. All content is reviewed for accuracy before publication. Sources include Cboe options reference materials, OCC publications, and standard financial texts.
