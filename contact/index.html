Contact

Research Inquiries

For questions about our market research, forecast methodology, or options strategy content, reach out through the contact channels below. We aim to respond to substantive research questions within 2-3 business days.

Partnership Opportunities

We occasionally explore co-publishing arrangements, data licensing, and strategic partnerships with financial media properties, research firms, and investment-focused platforms. Please include a brief description of the proposed collaboration in your initial outreach.

Media & Press

Media representatives seeking comment on market volatility, options strategy, or S&P 500 trend analysis should include publication details in their request. Note that all published research represents the entity's views only and should not be attributed to any third party without explicit confirmation.

Contact Channels

For general inquiries and research questions, use the contact information associated with Dependability Holdings LLC's registered business channels. All communications are subject to the disclaimer published on this site. Investment-related questions received through this site will not receive personalized responses — please consult a qualified financial advisor.

Response Time

We review inbound messages on business days. Typical response times: research questions 2-3 business days, partnership inquiries 1-2 weeks, media requests 1-2 business days. We do not provide trading advice, portfolio consultation, or individual investment recommendations through any contact channel.

Editorial contact

For corrections to published pieces, methodology questions, requests to expand on a specific concept, or general editorial inquiries, please email the editorial address listed in the site footer. We aim to respond within two business days. Verified corrections are appended to the bottom of the affected page with a dated correction note; minor corrections (typos, broken links) are made silently and noted in the next published post.

Partnership inquiries

For affiliate program inquiries, content partnerships, sponsored content proposals, and other commercial arrangements, please email the partnerships address with a brief description of the proposal. We do not currently accept sponsored content or paid placements on this site. We do participate in affiliate programs for tools and platforms we actually use; the affiliate relationships are disclosed on the relevant pages and do not influence the editorial content.

Press and media

Members of the press may reach out for comment, background interviews, or fact-checking. Please include your outlet, your deadline, and a brief description of the story. We respond to press inquiries within one business day when the deadline is within 48 hours. For longer-lead stories, we aim to respond within three business days. We do not provide pre-publication review of articles unless we are a named source.

Compliance and legal

DMCA notices, takedown requests, and other legal correspondence should be sent to the legal contact address with the subject line starting with "Legal:" so it is routed promptly. The publishing entity is Dependability Holdings LLC, a Nevada limited liability company. Service of process should be addressed to the registered agent in Nevada; the registered agent details are available from the Nevada Secretary of State.

Why no contact form

The site does not maintain a contact form because there is no backend to receive form submissions. All messages are routed through email. This is a deliberate choice: it eliminates the attack surface that comes with running a form-processing backend, and it makes the routing of different message types (editorial, partnership, press, legal) explicit and reliable. The email addresses are listed in the site footer.

For trading or investment questions, please consult a licensed professional — this site does not provide personalized advice. For technical issues with the site itself (broken pages, layout problems, accessibility issues), please email the editorial address with "site issue" in the subject line.
