Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Dependability Holdings LLC collects only standard server log information when you visit this website: IP address (stored anonymously), browser type, pages visited, and referring URL. This data is used solely for website security, analytics, and performance monitoring. We do not use tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or invasive analytics tools.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies for site functionality only — primarily session cookies for navigation state. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party profiling cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser without affecting your ability to read content on this site, though some interactive features may be limited.

How We Use Information

Information collected is used for: (1) operating and securing the website, (2) understanding aggregate traffic patterns to improve content and site performance, and (3) maintaining compliance with applicable laws and Cloudflare's service requirements. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

This site uses Cloudflare for content delivery and security, and Google AdSense for advertising. These services may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available at cloudflare.com/privacy. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Retention

Server log data is retained for a maximum of 30 days for security purposes, after which it is deleted or aggregated beyond identification. We do not maintain long-term databases of individual visitor behavior.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us through the channels described on our contact page. We will respond within 30 days. For EU/UK visitors, see our addendum below regarding GDPR and UK GDPR rights.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that personal information from a minor has been collected without verified parental consent, we will delete that information as soon as reasonably practicable.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any material changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage periodic review of this policy to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, reach out through our contact page. For GDPR/UK GDPR data requests, please include "Data Request" in your subject line so we can route your inquiry appropriately.

About this privacy policy

This privacy policy describes what information dependability.us collects when you visit the site, how that information is used, and the choices you have regarding the information. The policy applies to all pages on the dependability.us domain. The policy is incorporated by reference into any service offered on the site (newsletter subscriptions, contact forms, comment threads, and any other interactive features).

Information collected automatically

When you visit the site, standard technical information is collected through server logs and analytics tools. This includes your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, time spent on each page, and approximate geographic location (country and city-level, not precise). The information is collected through server logs (retained for 90 days) and through Google Analytics, a third-party analytics service governed by Google's privacy policy.

We use this information to understand how visitors use the site, to identify and fix technical problems, and to improve the content over time. We do not combine this information with personally identifiable information. We do not sell or share this information with third parties for their own purposes.

Cookies and similar technologies

The site uses cookies for two purposes. The first is functional: cookies remember your preferences (dark mode versus light mode) so the site can present the appropriate version on return visits. These cookies do not contain personal information and do not track you across other sites. The second is advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests and to measure ad performance. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings page.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies entirely may affect the functionality of the site (preferences may not be remembered) but will not prevent you from accessing the content. The site does not use any tracking technology beyond what is described in this policy.

Your rights and choices

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you; to request that we correct or delete your personal information; to opt out of marketing emails (via the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly); and to lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority in your jurisdiction. To exercise any of these rights, email the contact listed in the site footer. We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised. Material changes (changes that affect how we collect or use your information) will be announced in the next published post and, where appropriate, via email to newsletter subscribers. Non-material changes (clarifications, formatting, contact information updates) will be made without announcement.
